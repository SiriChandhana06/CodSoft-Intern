About Us


Welcome to IRIS Restaurant, where culinary artistry meets warm hospitality. We are more than just a restaurant; we are an experience, a place where the finest ingredients are transformed into delectable dishes that delight your senses.

Our Story
Founded in 2020, IRIS Restaurant has been a cornerstone of the local dining scene for Three years. Our journey began with a simple vision – to create a space where people could savor exceptional food in a cozy, inviting atmosphere. Over the years, we have grown and evolved, but our commitment to exceptional dining has remained unwavering.


Our Cuisine
At IRIS Restaurant, we take pride in our diverse menu, which is a reflection of our commitment to excellence. Our talented chefs craft each dish with precision and passion. Whether you're in the mood for traditional classics or daring new flavors, we have something for every palate.
The Dining Experience
Our restaurant is more than just a place to eat; it's a place to connect, celebrate, and create memories. The warm ambiance, attentive staff, and carefully curated wine and cocktail list set the stage for an unforgettable dining experience. From romantic dinners to family gatherings, IRIS Restaurant offers the perfect setting for any occasion.


Our Team
Our dedicated team is the heart and soul of IRIS Restaurant. From the kitchen to the front of the house, our staff is passionate about delivering top-notch service. We are here to make your dining experience exceptional, from the moment you step through our doors.
